Logo Slideshow is a nice Wordpress Slideshow for showing your Customer's Logos with Links.

== Description ==

Logo Slideshow provides a **powerfull** and nice javascript/DHTML Slideshow/Slider for Wordpress. It can be integrated anywhere on your Blog/Website (Looks best in Footer). This Logo Slideshow shows 6 of your most favourite Logos with a Link to their Homepage. This is perfect for everybody providing Webdesign or any other Service to show their references:

* 6 Logos
* Links with target=blank to Logo Homepage
* Very nice highlighting when hovering with mouse
* Administration Setting for Logo URL's and Paths
* Arrows for navigating through the Logos

Check out the <a href="http://www.iwebix.de/logo-slideshow-wordpress-plugin/" target="_blank">Plugin Page</a> and Demopage at <a href="http://www.iwebix.de" target="_blank">IWEBIX</a> in the Footer.

== Installation ==

1. Upload /logo-slideshow/ to your Plugin directory (wp-content/plugins/)
2. Go to the 'Plugins' Page of your Administration Area and activate the Plugin.
3. Go to Settings/Logo Slideshow and customize the Logo Paths and URL's.
3. Place `<?php include (ABSPATH . '/wp-content/plugins/logo-slideshow/logo-slider.php'); ?>`in your template.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I insert the Logo Slideshow? =

YOu can Insert the Logo Slideshow almost everywhere you want (it looks best in the Footer)!

= Where can I edit the Stylesheet? =

You can edit the width and height in /wp-content/plugins/logo-slideshow/css/image-slideshow.css

= What is perfect Resolution for the Images? =

You should have 70px as maximum for the height.
